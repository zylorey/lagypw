The Nether Portal Calculator is a tool designed to help Minecraft players link their portals correctly between the Overworld and the Nether dimensions.
Distance in the Nether is compressed by a ratio of 8:1 compared to the Overworld. This means every block you travel in the Nether is equivalent to 8 blocks in the Overworld.
To link portals precisely, you should divide your Overworld X and Z coordinates by 8 to get the corresponding Nether coordinates, or multiply Nether coordinates by 8 to get the Overworld ones.
The Y coordinate (height) does not change when calculating portal locations, but it is still important to ensure portals are at a safe and accessible altitude.
Use this calculator to quickly find where to build your portals for perfect synchronization and efficient travel across your Minecraft world.
For more info: minecraft.wiki/w/Nether_Portal
